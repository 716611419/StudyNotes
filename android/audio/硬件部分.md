# 高通平台 Audio codec简介
    高通codec分为内部codec和外部codec;内部codec以MSM8937/PM8940体系，外部codec以wcd9326;
    MSM8937/PM8940是以MSM8937做数字解码器，PM8940做混合信号编码器

# MI2S(与其他厂家芯片链接)
    MI2S在高通主控芯片上，通常有4路,分别为Primary、Secondary、Tertiary、Quarternary
Primary
    MI2S_1_MCLK – GPIO 64
    MI2S_1_SCK – GPIO 65
    MI2S_1_WS – GPIO 66
    MI2S_1_SD0 – GPIO 67
    MI2S_1_SD1 – GPIO 68
Secondary
    MI2S_2_MCLK – GPIO 78
    MI2S_2_SCK – GPIO 79
    MI2S_2_WS – GPIO 80
    MI2S_2_SD0 – GPIO 81
    MI2S_2_SD1 – GPIO 82
Tertiary
    MI2S_3_MCLK – GPIO 73
    MI2S_3_SCK – GPIO 74
    MI2S_3_WS – GPIO 75
    MI2S_3_SD0 – GPIO 76
    MI2S_3_SD1 – GPIO 77
Quarternary
    MI2S_4_MCLK – GPIO 57
    MI2S_4_SCK – GPIO 58
    MI2S_4_WS – GPIO 59
    MI2S_4_SD0 – GPIO 60
    MI2S_4_SD1 – GPIO 61
    MI2S_4_SD2 – GPIO 62
    MI2S_4_SD3 – GPIO 63
